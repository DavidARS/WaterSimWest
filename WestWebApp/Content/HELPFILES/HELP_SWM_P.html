Surface Water Management

The policy choice for "Surface Water" decreases or increases the amount of surface water available to be used by consumers that have access to surface water. The input buttons for this control range from "Least" (minimum amount of available surface water) to "Most" (maximum amount of available surface water). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Decrease the available Surface Water if you want to reduce stress on rivers and streams. Increase the available Surface Water if you find that no other options suit your needs.

NOTE:If the background color to the user input text box changes color, the surface water value has been adjusted by model to the value displayed: estimated demand is less than the available water supply.

In addition: 1) the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values for this policy, and 2) the model will NOT let you allocate more water than the estimated demand for water. It will adjust water supplies as needed.

Field name: SWM_P
