Drought Management

The policy control for Drought Distribution either maintains the default setting of "No Drought" or it implements and defines the severty of the drought. A drought is an extended period of time of reduced rainfall and, thus, decreased flows in rivers and streams. The input buttons for this control range from "None" (no drought) to "Extreme" (reductions in river flows that match the lowest records in the historical data).

Implementing a drought will reduce the flows on the rivers whereby decreasing the amount of surface water available for use by consumers.

Field name: DSCN_P
