Agricultural Efficiency of Water Use

The policy choice for Agriculture reduces the water demand for Agricultural Water Use. The input buttons for this control range from "No change" to "Extreme" change (very highly efficienct use of water by Agriculture). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Using less water means greater efficiency of water use by Agriculture (Farmers). That is, they can produce the same amount of crops, or maintain the same herd number, while using less water. Or, they can increase crop production or herd size while using the same amount of water as before.

NOTE: the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values.

Field name: ACON_P
