Cities and Towns Efficiency of Water Use

The policy choice for Cities and Towns reduces the water demand (water needs) for Cities and Towns. The input buttons for this control range from "No Change" to "Extreme" change (very highly efficienct use of water by Cities and Towns). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Using less water (each business or individual uses less water than before) by Cities and Towns means greater efficiency in the use of water by businesses and residents.

NOTE: the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values for this policy.

Field name: UCON_P
