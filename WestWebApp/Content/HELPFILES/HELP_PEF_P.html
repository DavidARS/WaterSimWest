Electricity Efficiency of Water Use Sustainability Indicator

The Electricity Efficiency of Water Use Indicator represents how well your power plants are managing their water.

If they are too efficient they will use less water but there will be increases in the cost of electricity for your community. If they are not efficient enough, their "lower water use technologies" could result in overuse of water with potential impacts on the long-term sustainability of your communties water supply.

Field name: PEF_P
