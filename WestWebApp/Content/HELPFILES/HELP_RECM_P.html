Reclaimed Water Management

The policy choice for Reclaimed Water looks different than other water source controls in that the value you select cannot exceed 100. This policy increases the amount of reclaimed water from Cities and Towns wastewater treatment plants. Some regions currently use no reclalined water while others use a lot. However, because the amount of reclaimed water is determined by the amount of treated wastewater produced, you cannot use more than 100% of that available (maximum amount produced).

The input buttons for this control range from "None" (no reclained water is used) to "All" (everything produced is used). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Increase your use of Reclaimed Water to add to surface and groundwater supplies.

NOTE: Two warnings: 1) the model will limit the upper boundary of viable input values for this control, and 2) as water demand increases more water is available to be reclaimed.

Field name: RECM_P
