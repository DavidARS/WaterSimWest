Industrial Efficiency of Water Use

The policy choice for Industry reduces the demand for water by Industry (for instance, mining for copper or coal, or other local industries that produce products for the local economy). The input buttons for this control range from "No Change" to "Extreme" change (very highly efficienct use of water by Industry). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Using less water means that mining companies or coal companies (or any local industry) are producing the same amount while using less water (or an increased amount while using the same amount of water as before).

NOTE: the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values for this control.

Field name: ICON_P
