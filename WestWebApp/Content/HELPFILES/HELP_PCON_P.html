Efficiency in Water Use for Electricity Production

The policy choice for Electricity Production changes the water demand (needs) for the production of electricity for distribution to your community. The input buttons for this control range from "No Change" to "Extreme" change (very highly efficienct use of water by electric utilities). To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Using less water (increasing efficiency) means reduced water use by the electric company while maintaining the same electrical generation.

NOTE: the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values for this policy.

Field name: PCON_P
