Population Growth Rate Management

The policy choice for Population Growth Rate policy decreases or increases the population of Cities and Towns. The input buttons for this control range from "Lowest" (minimum growth rate for the community) to "Higher" (maximum growth rate for the community). The "Planned" value represents that expected by your community leaders. To the right you will notice an input box that enables the user to enter a desired value to use.

Decrease population to reduce overall demand for water. Increase population to enable new people to move into the area.

NOTE: the model will limit the lower and upper boundaries of viable input values.

Field name: POPGRM_P
